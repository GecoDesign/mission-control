üìö Cold Email Best Practices

‚úÖ Email Outreach Tips

  • Personalize every email: Reference their company, recent work, or specific challenges in their industry.
  • Keep it short: Aim for 50-125 words. Busy professionals don't read long emails.
  • Focus on value: What's in it for them? Lead with benefits, not features.
  • Clear CTA: One specific call-to-action. Make it easy to respond.
  • Follow up: 80% of sales require 5+ follow-ups. Don't give up after one email.
  • Timing matters: Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10AM-11AM or 2PM-3PM for best response rates.
  • A/B test subject lines: Try different angles to see what resonates.
  • Warm up your domain: Don't send 100 emails on day one. Ramp up gradually.

üîí GDPR Compliance Checklist

  • Legitimate interest: You can email businesses without consent if you have a legitimate business interest.
  • Easy opt-out: Include clear unsubscribe language in every email.
  • Accurate data: Only contact people whose details you've obtained legally.
  • Respect objections: If someone asks not to be contacted, honor that immediately.
  • Data protection: Store prospect data securely (this tool uses local browser storage).
  • B2B exception: GDPR is more lenient for B2B emails to corporate email addresses.

‚è∞ Optimal Timing

  • Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
  • Worst days: Monday (inbox overload), Friday (weekend mode)
  • Best times: 10-11 AM (after morning email check), 2-3 PM (afternoon lull)
  • Follow-up cadence: Wait 3-4 days between emails, then 5-7 days for later follow-ups

üí¨ Response Templates

If they're interested:

"Thanks for getting back to me! I'd love to learn more about [specific challenge]. Are you free for a quick 15-minute call next week? Here's my calendar: [link]"

If they need more info:

"Happy to share more details. Here are a few recent projects we've done for [similar companies]: [portfolio link]. Would you like me to send over a case study specific to [their industry]?"

If timing isn't right:

"No problem at all. When would be a better time to reconnect? I'll put a reminder to follow up in [3 months/6 months]."
